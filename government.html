Why Governments Choose Linux

Digital Sovereignty

Control your own digital infrastructure. No dependence on foreign corporations or closed-source software. Complete independence in technology decisions.

Example: French Gendarmerie achieved complete digital independence

Massive Cost Savings

Eliminate license fees for operating systems and productivity software. Redirect taxpayer money to public services instead of software corporations.

Example: Munich saved €10M+ migrating to Linux

Enhanced Security

Open source means vulnerabilities are found and fixed quickly. No backdoors, no forced telemetry. Security agencies can audit every line of code.

Example: US Department of Defense uses Linux for security

Government Migration Strategy

Phase 1

Assessment & Planning

  • Inventory existing hardware and software
  • Identify mission-critical applications
  • Develop compatibility testing procedures
  • Create training plans for staff
Phase 2

Pilot Deployment

  • Select non-critical department for pilot
  • Deploy dual-boot or test machines
  • Provide intensive training and support
  • Gather feedback and adjust approach
Phase 3

Gradual Rollout

  • Migrate department by department
  • Maintain parallel systems during transition
  • Establish Linux support team
  • Develop custom solutions as needed
Phase 4

Full Deployment & Optimization

  • Complete migration of all systems
  • Optimize configurations for government needs
  • Establish open source contribution policies
  • Share experiences with other governments
